Work

In the sixth grade, me nd my closest friend at the time worked together to find a better solution to plastic. We were able to make plastic out of milk and vinegar and made it to nationals in the science fair.

In the seventh grade I participated in Ecybermission which is a nationwide competition for stdents who are interested in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) and use their understanding of those topics to solve a problem. My team and I (Grace and Shreya) worked on a better design of house-foundation to prevent future hurricane disasters, we made it to nationals for the town-wide science fair. In the eigth grade, my team and I (Avanthika and Jessica) searched for better ways to insulate houses; we got first place in our age group in our state for Ecybermission. We also got a one-thousand dollar prize each.

Art is one of my favorite hobbies. Last year, I submitted two pieces into the Scholastic Art and Writing Contest. I received two honorabble mentions for my work. This year, I submitted one of them and it was nominated to be published into a book (final choice still pending).
